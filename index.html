Pomegranates

The Fruit of the Dead

Read More

pōmum grānātum

The name "Pomegranate" means "Seedy Apple" in Latin. They can contain anywhere from 200-1400 seeds per fruit.

A Super Fruit?

The super fruit designation comes from the fact that pomegranate juice is super high in antioxidants.

Health Benefits

Studies show that it can protect the heart in many ways, like lowering blood pressure and reducing blood sugar levels.
